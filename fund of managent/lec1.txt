[cite_start]A manager coordinates and oversees the work of others to achieve organizational goals.

Page 2: Efficiency, Effectiveness, and Functions
Manager-Employee Relationships

[cite_start]Positive manager-employee relationships significantly boost performance, loyalty, and retention.   

[cite_start]This leads to higher engagement and profitability.   

Defining Management [cite_start]Management is coordinating work activities of others to achieve EFFICIENT and EFFECTIVE outcomes.   

[cite_start]

Efficiency: Doing things right (using minimal resources/time/effort to maximize output).   

[cite_start]

Effectiveness: Doing the right things (actions that align with organizational benefits/goals and the degree to which objectives are achieved).

[cite_start]

[cite_start]

Leading: Motivating and engaging people.   

[cite_start]

Controlling: Monitoring progress and making corrections.
