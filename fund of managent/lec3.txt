Bounded Rationality:[cite_start]Decision-making within cognitive limits. [cite_start]Aims for "good enough" rather than perfect. [cite_start]Used when information is limited and needs assumptions. Intuition:[cite_start]Based on experience, feelings, judgment, and unconscious reasoning. [cite_start]Useful when data is limited or in times of uncertainty. [cite_start]Can be biased if not kept in check, but useful under time pressure.
